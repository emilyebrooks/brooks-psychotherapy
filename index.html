Why Cancer Counseling?

Cancer affects individuals and families in numerous ways and cancer counseling provides specialized support through the changes and impact of dealing with cancer. While some level of distress is considered normal and expected, patients often report that they are reluctant to turn to family for support, leaving many feeling isolated and misunderstood. When this occurs, such individuals will benefit from seeking professional help and cancer counseling.
